jQuery Functions

.click()

Click button to turn its text red. Click again to return to black.

.hide()

Click button to hide all the p tags.

.show()

Click button to show all the p tags.

.toggle()

Click button to toggle display of section title.

.slideDown()

Click button to slide down page title.

.slideUp()

Click button to slide up page title.

.slideToggle()

Click button to slide all h1 tags up or down.

.fadeIn()

Click button to fade in a ghost.

.fadeOut()

Click button to fade me out.

.addClass()

Click button to add some class to the section titles.

.before()

Click button to add element(s) before me.

.after()

Click button to add element(s) after me.

.append()

Click button to append to me.

.html()

Click button to get html. Click me to set html.

.attr()

Click section title to add attribute. Click button to see its ID attribute:

.val()

.text()

Click button to change my text.
